Core Beliefs

Faith anchored in Scripture.

Our convictions are shaped by the Bible, the historic Adventist message, and a commitment to share hope with every student.

Convictions

Rooted in the Bible and Adventist truth.

We seek to understand God’s Word, honor the Sabbath, and live with hope for the second coming of Christ.

01

The Bible

The Bible is the inspired Word of God and the foundation for faith and practice.

02

The Sabbath

Saturday is celebrated as a day of rest, worship, and spiritual renewal.

03

Christ’s Ministry

Jesus is our Savior, Redeemer, and Lord, fully God and fully human.

04

Salvation

Grace through faith in Christ gives assurance, forgiveness, and new life.

05

Second Coming

We believe Jesus will return visibly and that this hope shapes how we live today.

06

Mission

God calls us to share the gospel through worship, service, and loving witness.

Practice

Living faith with clarity and compassion.

Our beliefs guide worship, discipleship, community care, and how we engage with life on campus and beyond.

Find strength in scripture

Every gathering at CukSDA honors the Bible as our source of truth and the life-changing message of Jesus Christ.
