What We Believe

Anchored in Scripture.

Our convictions flow from the Bible — the inspired Word of God — and the historic teachings of the Seventh-day Adventist Church.

01

The Bible

The Scriptures are the inspired Word of God and the authoritative source of doctrine and life.

02

The Sabbath

Saturday — the seventh day — is God's gift of rest, worship, and reconnection with our Creator.

03

Jesus Christ

Fully God and fully human, Jesus is our Savior, Lord, and soon-returning King.

04

The Second Coming

Christ's return in glory is the blessed hope and climax of the gospel — literal, personal, visible.
