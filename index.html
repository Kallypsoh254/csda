Welcome Home

You are deeply welcome here.

Whether you are a lifelong Adventist or simply curious about faith, CukSDA is a community shaped by grace — open to all, committed to truth.

We exist to connect the entire CUK campus community with the love, hope, and truth found in Jesus Christ. Our church life is built around the Sabbath — from Sabbath School at 9:00 AM to the Divine Service at 11:00 AM — but our community extends through the whole week with prayer meetings, Bible studies, ministry projects, and campus outreach.

With over 200 active members and eight thriving ministries, CukSDA has been a place of encounter, growth, and transformation since our founding. Come and see for yourself.

What We Believe

Anchored in Scripture,
united in truth.

Our faith rests on the 28 Fundamental Beliefs of the Seventh-day Adventist Church — a comprehensive biblical framework covering God, salvation, the church, and Christian living.

Explore All 28 Beliefs
Belief 01

Holy Scriptures

The 66 books of the Bible are the inspired, inerrant Word of God and the only rule of faith and practice.

Belief 05

The Trinity

One God exists as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — three co-eternal persons perfectly united in nature and purpose.

Belief 10

Salvation in Christ

By grace through faith alone, humanity is justified, sanctified, and glorified through Jesus Christ.

Belief 20

The Sabbath

Saturday — the seventh day — is God's holy rest, a weekly sign of our covenant relationship with our Creator.

Belief 24

The Second Coming

Christ's literal, personal, visible return is the blessed hope — the grand climax of the gospel message.

Belief 14

Unity in the Body

In Christ all barriers are broken. The church is one body — diverse in expression, united in mission and love.

Belief 22

Christian Stewardship

God is owner of all. We are stewards of time, talent, and treasure — returning tithes and offerings with joy.

Belief 28

The New Earth

God will re-create the earth as the eternal home of the redeemed — where sin, death, and sorrow are no more.
