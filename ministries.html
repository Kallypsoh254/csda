Our Ministries

Ministry that serves campus life.

From worship and outreach to youth fellowship and health ministry, CukSDA supports every student through meaningful service and shared mission.

Connected Service

Ministries designed for every season of student life.

Each ministry team helps students grow spiritually, connect with others, and serve the university and surrounding community.

Worship & Devotion

Leading Sabbath services, devotional studies, and worship preparation that center our community on Christ.

Youth & University

Helping students build friendships, plan AY programs, and create spaces for fun and faith to grow together.

Health & Wellness

Promoting whole-person health through seminars, wellness days, and lifestyle education.

Evangelism & Outreach

Sharing the hope of Jesus through campus events, community drives, and volunteer outreach.

Discipleship & Growth

Offering Bible study groups, mentorship circles, and spiritual growth pathways for all students.

Volunteer Support

Creating easy ways to serve — from meal distribution to peer tutoring and campus care projects.

Why Join

Get involved and make a difference.

Being part of a ministry team is one of the best ways to grow spiritually, develop leadership skills, and build relationships that last beyond campus.
