Our Ministry Family

More than programs —
a place to belong.

Each ministry at CukSDA is a living community of students, staff, and friends who are growing in faith, serving one another, and reaching out to the campus around us.

Whether you are passionate about music, health, prayer, or evangelism — or you are simply looking for a home — there is a ministry here shaped for your gifts and your season of life. We believe every believer is gifted, and every gift has a role in God's mission at CUK.

Our ministries meet throughout the week, connecting faith with everyday campus life. Find your people. Find your purpose.

Ministry Spotlight

Evangelism & Outreach — at the heart of our calling.

More than any programme, evangelism is a posture — the willingness to step outside your comfort zone and share the hope of Christ with someone who has not yet heard it. At CukSDA, we train, equip, and send members to do exactly that.

  • Annual Campus Evangelistic Series drawing 500+ attendees
  • Door-to-door literature distribution every school term
  • Partnership with East Kenya Union Conference
  • Student evangelism training and public speaking workshops

Get Involved

Three simple steps
to find your place.

Joining a ministry at CukSDA is straightforward — no auditions, no prerequisites. Just show up with a willing heart.

1

Come on a Sabbath

Attend any Saturday service and stay for the ministry fair table near the foyer. Our leaders will be there to meet you and tell you about their work.

2

Speak to a Ministry Leader

Connect directly with the leader of the ministry you are drawn to. They will answer your questions, introduce you to the team, and invite you to the next gathering.

3

Show Up & Serve

Attend your first meeting. Serve once. Then see whether it feels like home. Most people know right away — and we think you will too.

✉

Or Contact Us Online

Not yet ready to come in person? Send us a message through our contact page and we will connect you with the right ministry leader directly.

"I was terrified my first Sabbath. By the second, I was helping lead the prayer team."

Grace Wanjiku · Prayer Ministry

Ready to Serve?

Your gifts are welcome here.

Whether you have been a member for years or this is your very first look at CukSDA — there is a place for you in one of our eight ministries. We do not require experience; we simply ask for a willing heart.
