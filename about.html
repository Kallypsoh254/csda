Who We Are

Welcome to CukSDA

We are a Seventh-day Adventist student community at Cooperative University of Kenya, rooted in worship, fellowship, and service.

Mission

Our purpose is to grow together in faith and impact.

At CukSDA, we gather to study Scripture, encourage one another, and serve our campus. Our gatherings, outreach, and discipleship are shaped by the Adventist call to share Christ’s love with every student.

History

Built by students, for students.

Founded as a fellowship group at CUK, our community has grown into a trusted campus ministry. From weekly Sabbath services to prayer nights and outreach events, every season has deepened our commitment to one another and to God.

  • Weekly worship and Bible study
  • Student-led prayer groups
  • Community outreach projects
  • Mentoring and discipleship teams
Leadership

Guided by servant leaders.

Our leadership team is made up of student officers, campus pastors, and ministry coordinators. Together they ensure every event, group, and service opportunity is welcoming, organized, and spiritually nourishing.

Student President

Leading with compassion and a heart for campus community.

Prayer Coordinator

Connecting our members through intercession and spiritual care.

Ministry Director

Designing outreach, worship, and service opportunities across campus.

Join Us

Everyone is welcome to explore faith and friendship.

Whether you are already an Adventist student or simply curious about Christ, you will find a warm welcome here. Join our Saturday worship, prayer nights, small groups, and service teams.
